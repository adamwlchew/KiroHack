# PageFlow AI Learning Platform

PageFlow is a cross-platform AI-powered learning platform that operates across web, mobile AR, and VR environments. It provides personalized, accessible, and engaging educational experiences through a microservices architecture built on AWS.

## Core Features

- **Cross-Platform Experience**: Seamless access across web, mobile AR, and VR platforms
- **AI Companion "Page"**: An engaging character that guides users through their learning journey
- **Comprehensive Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with extensive accessibility features
- **Curriculum Alignment**: Content mapped to educational standards (Australian, UK, US, etc.)
- **AI-Powered Learning**: Personalized learning paths and adaptive content
- **Progress Tracking**: Comprehensive tracking of user achievements and learning milestones

## Target Users

- **Learners**: Students of all ages and abilities seeking personalized education
- **Educators**: Teachers and instructors who want to align content with curriculum standards
- **Developers**: Team members building and extending the platform

## Key Principles

- **Accessibility First**: Design for all users regardless of abilities
- **Cross-Platform Consistency**: Maintain content and experience consistency across platforms
- **AI-Enhanced Learning**: Use AI to personalize and improve the learning experience
- **Microservices Architecture**: Independent, scalable services for different platform functions
